Black and White Photography

Black and white photography is a style where the color spectrum is removed from the photo, leaving only the shades of black and white. Distractions are removed in black and white colors like vibrant objects, captivating backgrounds, or things that are not part of the subject. Black and white photography also brings our mood to a different level. It often depicts power, elegance, sophistication, status, formality, evil, death, mystery, grief, mourning, the occult, bleakness, heaviness, depression, rebellion, and fear.

A Glimpse in the World of Black and White

Our world is colorful. But we wanted to look for something different. The colors are amazing and we do love colors to the bone. But black and white photography relax us. It feels like a newfound world for us. Everything can be sad or elegant, happy or freaky, life or death, love or sorrow. It is only a matter of perspective and understanding by the viewer or artist.
